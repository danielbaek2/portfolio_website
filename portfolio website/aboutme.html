About Me

Hi, my name is Daniel Eungyeol Baek, and I am currently a third-year computer science student studying at the Rochester Institute of Technology for my Bachelor's Degree in science.

Aside from coding, I am an adamand basketball fan. I enjoy playing with my friends and watching my favorite team, the Houston Rockets. It should be noted that the outcome of their games directly impacts my mood for the next couple of hours. When Game 7 of the 2018 Western Conference Finals is mentioned, I get extreme PTSD and will become very sad in response to such horrid memories. Generally though, for most other sports, I am a casual sports fan and only watch when it is on the television, like football or the World Cup as a couple of examples. While I won't be as engaged when talking about basketball, I will likely be aware of what's taking place in the specific league.

My main passion/hobby is art, however, whether it be drawing or creating videos. When I was younger, I always wanted to work in the film industry and, for the longest time, animation was my dream, seeming as I could intertwine my love for drawing with my love for filmmaking; however, as the industry declined with the pandemic, I transitioned into something more manageable and future-proof, like computer science. Now, I'm creating videos to scratch this itch I still have, making video essays about films that I love and, eventually, short films that I can proudly share with the world.
